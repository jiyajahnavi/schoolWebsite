Our Aim

This school is established with the view of fulfilment of today's need of modern education aiming at a harmonious development of the students physical, mental and moral power with emphasis on character building and growth of an integrated personality. We help to learn how to establish good human relationship imbibe virtues of moral courage and social discipline. We aim at fostering among the students a spirit of service to others Irrespective of caste, creed, colair, community or nationality. We help to develop scientific outlook in the pursuit to the students' studies. We help to acquire economic understanding and efficiency in the management of expenditure. We aspire to create a dynamic learning community that emphasizes academic excellence, critical thinking, and personal growth. Our goal is to nurture well-rounded individuals who are not only knowledgeable but also ethical, compassionate, and socially responsible. By integrating modern teaching methodologies with state-of-the-art facilities, we aim to provide a stimulating and supportive atmosphere that encourages students to explore their potential and pursue their passions. We believe in the power of education to transform lives and are committed to equipping our students with the skills and knowledge necessary to succeed in an ever-evolving world. Our dedicated faculty, innovative curriculum, and diverse extracurricular programs work in harmony to ensure that each student receives a comprehensive education that prepares them for the challenges and opportunities of the future. At the heart of our vision is a commitment to continuous improvement and adaptability, recognizing the need to evolve with the times while maintaining our core values. We strive to be a beacon of educational excellence, inspiring a lifelong love of learning and a dedication to making a positive impact in the world.

Main Features

1. Tuition is given by all highly qualified, well-trained, technically sound, experiericed and dedicated teachers.
2. Medium of teaching in school is English and Hindi and encouragement to the study of Sanskrit.
3. Hobbies and Co-curricular Activities for all-round development of kids. Le. Yoga/Meditation, Painting, Photography, Gardening, Athletics, Clay Mode Sports Public Speaking Drama, Debates, Educational Tours and Social Service Activities.
4. All class-rooms are well furnished.
5. Best, economical and specific books are prescribed, so that students should not feel over burdened.
6. No home work will be given to NURSERY class students.
7. School provides competitive environment to all students & attractive awards/rewards/prizes, so that they should get themselves motivated towards study.
8. Regular classes are held for five days in a week. Saturday is reserved for tests and co-curricular activities. Sunday is the weekly holiday.
