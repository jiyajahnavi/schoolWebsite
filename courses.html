Admission Rules

1. Registration/Admission forms for new admission are available from first week of January every year at the school counter on the cash payment of Rs.500/-
2. Selection is made on the basis of counselling session held on 26th of January every year to determine the capability of the kids to pursue the prescribed courses of studies
3. For admission to PRE-NURSERY the kid should complete the age of 2% years on first April of the year of admission.
4. At the time of Admission you have to deposit following documents
(i)Transfer Certificate
(ii)Character Certificate
(iii)Mark Sheet
(iv)Birth Certificate

Withdrawal Procedure

1. One month's notice or one month's fee in lieu there of is necessary in case a parent/guardian wants to withdrawal his/her ward from the school
2 . For withdrawal of conveyance facility one month's notice is necessary.
3. A no dues certificate is required to be obtained from all the departments of the school for seeking withdrawal.
4. Withdrawal/Transfer in month of December will not be accepted.

Evaluation

1st Term: April to September
SA 1 in September
2nd Term: October to March
SA 2 in March
Project FAs and other evaluations and observations will be conducted as per C.B.S.E. guidelines.

Rules of the School

1. Students must come to school regularly ten minutes before the moming bell rings. They should come to school in proper uniform as per the specification and design laid by the school.
2. if a students is absent, explanation for absence must be written in ink in the school diary and signed by the parents/guardians. No one who has been absent on the previous day may be admitted to class without the permission of the principal/leave-in-charge.
3. 80% attendance of the students is required in order to appear in any of the examination held in the school. The maximum attendance is also necessary for the overall progress of the students.
4. Students returning to school after an infection or contagious disease must produce Doctor's certificate permitting him to rejoin class.
5. All students must attend class on the opening day after each of the vacations and on the closing day before the vacations and otherwise a fine of Rs. 5/- per day will have to be charged.
6. Students are not allowed to bring or wear any valuable article in the school. If they do so, the school authorities will not be responsible for the same.
7. No present of any kind should be given to any teacher/staff
8.The teachers are not permitted to take home Tuition of any student without prior written permission of the Director/Principal Any infringement of this rule will be seriously dealt with
9.School owns the right to take severe action even to remove from the school if a student is found guilty of misconduct. Misconduct means:

(i) Disfiguring or damaging of any school property
(ii) Smoking
(iii)Any form of gambling.
(iv) Use of drugs & intoxicants.
(v) Rowdyism and rude behaviour
(vi) Use of violence in any room/school campus/bus
(vii) Casteism Communalism or practice of untouchability.
(viii) Not following the Examination Rules property

10. School authority reserves its right to modify, alter and or include any other rules/terms and conditions that may deemed fit in the interest of the school,
